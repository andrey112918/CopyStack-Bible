# CopyStack Bible

## Overview

The CopyStack Bible is the central knowledge base for the entire CopyStack ecosystem.

It documents the vision, architecture, engineering decisions, products, standards, and long-term direction of the company.

Rather than serving as traditional documentation, the Bible acts as the permanent source of truth that guides every technical and business decision.

---

# Mission

CopyStack is building an AI Employee Operating System capable of automating complex creative and business workflows.

Instead of developing isolated AI tools, the ecosystem is composed of specialized AI Employees that collaborate to solve real-world problems.

Every product shares the same architectural principles:

* modular AI Employees
* explainable AI
* reusable components
* shared intelligence
* typed communication
* plugin-based extensibility

---

# Current Product

## CopyStack Video Engine

The Video Engine is the first product built on top of the AI Employee Operating System.

Its objective is to automate professional cinematic video editing through specialized AI Employees capable of understanding:

* speech
* story
* music
* visual assets
* editing strategy
* audience retention

Every creative decision is generated before rendering begins and can be inspected through Explain Mode.

# Current Architecture

The Video Engine currently follows this workflow:

```
Speech

↓

Speech Analysis

↓

Story Intelligence

↓

AI Employees

↓

Timeline Planner

↓

Motion Intelligence

↓

Director Brain

↓

Audit Layer

↓

Explain Engine

↓

RenderPlan

↓

Renderer

↓

Export
```

This architecture separates AI reasoning from rendering and provides complete explainability for every important decision.

# Design Philosophy

CopyStack follows several permanent engineering principles.

* AI-first architecture
* Explainable AI
* Modular systems
* Test-driven development
* Plugin-based extensibility
* Long-term maintainability

These principles ensure that every new technology introduced into the ecosystem remains reusable, scalable, and easy to evolve.

---

# Philosophy

The goal of CopyStack is not simply to automate software.

The goal is to build specialized AI Employees capable of understanding, reasoning, collaborating, and continuously improving through shared knowledge and reusable architecture.

Every architectural decision documented in this repository should move the ecosystem one step closer to that vision.
