The immediate objective is to evolve CopyStack from deterministic intelligence into reusable institutional knowledge.

1. Knowledge Layer
2. Gold Dataset Platform
3. Knowledge Graph
4. Shared AI Memory

These capabilities will become the foundation for every future AI Employee inside the CopyStack ecosystem.
