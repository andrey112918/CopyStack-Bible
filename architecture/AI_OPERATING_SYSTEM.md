# CopyStack AI Operating System

## Purpose

CopyStack is not just a video editor.

CopyStack is an AI Creative Studio made of specialized AI employees that collaborate to create videos with stronger retention, clarity, and conversion.

The goal is not to add effects randomly.

The goal is to decide why an effect should exist, when it should appear, and how it supports the message.

---

## Core Principle

Renderer does not think.

Renderer executes.

All intelligence must happen before rendering.

---

## System Levels

### Level 1: AI Kernel

The AI Kernel is the orchestration layer.

Responsibilities:

- Build the Video Context
- Send the context to all AI Directors
- Collect their decisions
- Resolve conflicts
- Produce the final Creative Decision
- Send execution instructions to employees

---

### Level 2: Video Genome

The Video Genome describes the DNA of the video.

It contains:

- Story DNA
- Marketing DNA
- Emotion DNA
- Retention DNA
- Visual DNA
- Brand DNA
- Platform DNA

---

### Level 3: Video Context

Video Context is the source of truth for all directors.

It contains:

- transcript
- current sentence
- previous sentence
- next sentence
- industry
- platform
- goal
- language
- story phase
- emotion
- retention score
- marketing goal
- editing report
- current scene index
- current sentence index

---

### Level 4: Creative Council

The Creative Council is made of specialized AI Directors.

Directors:

- Creative Director
- Emotion Director
- Story Director
- Marketing Director
- Retention Director
- Psychology Director
- Visual Director
- Camera Director
- Brand Director
- Platform Director

Each director has one responsibility.

No director should directly call another director.

All communication goes through the AI Kernel.

---

### Level 5: Creative Decision

Creative Decision is the final output of the council.

It answers:

- What is the goal of this sentence?
- What emotion should it create?
- How important is it?
- What camera movement should be used?
- What subtitle style should be used?
- What motion should be used?
- What assets should appear?
- What SFX should be used?
- Why?

---

### Level 6: AI Employees

Employees execute decisions.

Examples:

- Design Director
- Scene Director
- Subtitle Director
- Motion Director
- B-Roll Director
- Asset Manager

Employees should not decide the strategy.

They execute the Creative Decision.

---

### Level 7: Renderer

The Renderer converts the final RenderPlan into video.

It should not make creative decisions.

---
