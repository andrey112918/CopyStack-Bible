# Report Protocol

## Objective

Every AI Employee communicates through structured reports.

Reports are immutable once generated and are consumed by downstream systems.

---

## Report Requirements

Every report must include:

- source
- timestamp
- confidence
- reason
- warnings
- metadata
- version

---

- selected_effects
- reason
- warnings
