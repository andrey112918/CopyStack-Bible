## Problems Encountered

### Module Coordination

Previously, several intelligence modules operated independently, making it difficult to maintain a consistent execution order.

The new pipeline architecture introduced a unified orchestration layer responsible for coordinating every analysis stage.

---

### Shared Context

Multiple analysis modules required access to the same business information.

A shared execution context was introduced to eliminate duplicated processing and improve consistency across intelligence modules.

---

### Pipeline Extensibility

The Outreach Engine is expected to continue growing significantly.

The pipeline architecture was therefore designed to support future intelligence modules without requiring major structural changes.

---

## Decisions Made

### Pipeline Before Features

A strategic decision was made to prioritize execution architecture before implementing additional intelligence modules.

A stable pipeline provides a significantly stronger foundation for future development than adding isolated features.

---

### Shared Intelligence Context

Every pipeline stage should consume and enrich the same shared intelligence context instead of generating isolated outputs.

This improves consistency while reducing duplicated computation.

---

### Unified Business Reports

All business analysis should ultimately contribute to a single structured intelligence report.

Future recommendation systems will build upon this unified representation rather than independent module outputs.
