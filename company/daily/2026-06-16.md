# Daily Development Log — 2026-06-16

## Main Focus

Today the primary objective was to transform the Video Engine from a simple subtitle renderer into a modular editing pipeline.

## Completed Work

### Subtitle Synchronization

Improved subtitle synchronization using Whisper word timestamps.

The subtitle system now follows spoken words much more accurately, allowing subtitles to appear and disappear in sync with the speaker instead of using approximate sentence timing.

### Subtitle Intelligence

Expanded the subtitle pipeline to identify important keywords inside sentences.

Instead of displaying only full subtitles, the system can now separate:

* Text before the keyword
* The keyword itself
* Text after the keyword

This allows visual emphasis to be applied independently.

### Subtitle Composer

Implemented the Subtitle Composer responsible for splitting subtitles around important keywords while preserving punctuation and sentence structure.

Special handling was added for Romanian compound words such as:

* ENGINE-UL

preventing incorrect subtitle splitting.

### Video Analyst

Started the first version of the Video Analyst employee.

The employee now analyses speech and generates editing recommendations instead of relying only on subtitle timing.

Current responsibilities include:

* Important words
* Important phrases
* Important sentences
* Editing moments

### Editing Pipeline

Defined the first modular editing pipeline:

Speech Analysis

↓

Video Analyst

↓

Edit Planner

↓

Timeline Builder

↓

Renderer

This separates analysis from rendering and prepares the engine for future AI employees.

## Problems Encountered

* Subtitle timing needed several iterations before reaching acceptable precision.
* Compound Romanian words required additional normalization logic.

## Decisions Made

* Subtitle timing will always be based on word timestamps whenever available.
* Subtitle rendering should remain independent from editing decisions.
* AI Employees should generate instructions rather than render effects directly.

* Begin building the Asset-Based Motion System.
* Improve visual editing quality.
