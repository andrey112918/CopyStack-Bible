## Decisions Made

### Session-Centric Architecture

A strategic decision was made to organize runtime execution around persistent research sessions instead of isolated processing jobs.

This provides a scalable foundation for future dataset management and collaborative research workflows.

---

### Unified Runtime Lifecycle

Every Research Session now follows the same lifecycle regardless of processing complexity.

Maintaining a consistent execution model simplifies future extensions and improves long-term maintainability.

---

### Synchronization Before Parallelism

Before introducing more advanced execution strategies, workspace synchronization and runtime consistency must remain the highest priority.

Ensuring deterministic execution is more valuable than increasing processing throughput during the current development stage.
