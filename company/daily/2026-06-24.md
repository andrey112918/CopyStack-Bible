# Day 5 — Audit Layer & Explain Mode Foundation

## Goal

Complete the Explainable AI foundation for CopyStack Video Engine by introducing a full Audit Layer and the first version of Explain Mode.

---

# Completed

## Audit Layer

Implemented the complete auditing infrastructure used by every AI employee.

Components:

* AuditEntry
* AuditReport
* AuditContext
* DecisionLogger
* AuditExporter

The Audit Layer now records every important AI decision during video generation.
