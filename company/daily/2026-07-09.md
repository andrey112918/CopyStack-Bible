# Problems Encountered

## Media Profile Limitations

Current Media Profile still relies on placeholder-level probing.

The following metadata is not consistently populated:

- Duration
- FPS
- Resolution
- Width
- Height
- Video Codec
- Audio Codec

Although downstream modules already obtain enough information to continue processing, Media Profile should become the single source of truth.

---

## Transcript Extraction

Current Transcript Foundation validates architecture only.

Manual registration works correctly.

Automatic speech extraction has intentionally been postponed to a future phase.

---

## Localhost Scope

Research Center currently validates one source at a time.

Multi-source workflows and batch research sessions remain future work.

---

# Decisions Made

## Research Before Intelligence

A strategic decision was made to complete the entire Research Automation infrastructure before implementing AI reasoning.

This reduces future refactoring and creates reusable building blocks.

---

## Validation Before Expansion

Instead of immediately implementing Motion Analysis and Brand DNA, a dedicated validation sprint was completed.

This ensures the existing foundation is stable before additional complexity is introduced.

---

## Research Center Philosophy

Research Center is not intended to become a video analyzer only.

Its long-term purpose is to become the knowledge acquisition system of the CopyStack ecosystem.

Future products will consume its outputs rather than re-analyzing media independently.

---

## Modular Architecture
