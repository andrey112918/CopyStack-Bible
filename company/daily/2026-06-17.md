# Video Engine Progress

## Subtitle System

* Improved subtitle synchronization using Whisper word timestamps.
* Fixed subtitle splitting around important keywords.
* Fixed handling of compound words such as "ENGINE-UL".
* Subtitle rendering is now significantly more accurate.

## Video Analyst

Expanded the Video Analyst employee.

The system can now detect:

* Important words
* Important phrases
* Important sentences
* Editing moments
* Editing recommendations

The Video Analyst became the main intelligence layer that decides which parts of the speech deserve visual emphasis.

## Motion Overlay System

Started replacing FFmpeg drawbox-based motion graphics with a real asset pipeline.

Implemented:

* Motion Director
* Motion Asset Database
* Overlay Renderer

Motion graphics are now synchronized with subtitle keyword overlays instead of random transcript positions.

Current flow:

Speech Analysis

↓

Video Analyst

↓

Subtitle Director

↓

Motion Director

↓

Asset Database

↓

Overlay Renderer

↓

Renderer

## Overlay Assets

Created the first version of an asset-based motion system.

Current assets:

* Underline
* Highlight Box
* Impact Frame
* CTA Frame
* Top Flash

Assets are loaded dynamically from the assets/motion directory.

## Renderer

Renderer now supports:

* filter_complex
* multiple FFmpeg inputs
* PNG overlay assets

This prepares the renderer for:

* PNG overlays
* WebM overlays
* MOV overlays
* Blender exports
* 3D assets
