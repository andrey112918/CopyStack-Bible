# Daily Development Log

## Main Focus

Continue development of the CopyStack Localhost Outreach Engine and transform it from a business discovery tool into an intelligent outreach workspace.

---

## Completed Work

### Prospect Analysis Engine V1

Implemented the first intelligence layer between Business Discovery and future outreach generation.

Added:

- Business Analyzer
- Website Analyzer
- Contact Analyzer
- Website Health Analyzer
- Problem Detector
- Business Needs Engine
- Business Intent Engine
- Opportunity Detector
- Score Calculator
- Priority Calculator
- Confidence Calculator
- Evidence Builder
- Recommendation Generator

Created a reusable ProspectAnalysisReport that provides structured, deterministic and evidence-backed business intelligence.

---

### Localhost Integration

Integrated Prospect Analysis directly into the discovery workflow.

Each discovered prospect now receives:

- Overall Score
- Priority
- Confidence
- Business Intent
- Recommended Service
- Outreach Angle
- Problems
- Opportunities
- Evidence Summary
- Readiness indicators

---

### Outreach Engine UI/UX V2

Redesigned the Prospect Workspace.

Major improvements:

- Decision-first interface
- Hero Summary
- Command Center
- Business Health overview
- Top Problems
- Top Opportunities
- Opportunity Plan
- Quick Wins
- Collapsible intelligence sections
- Reduced visual clutter

## Problems Encountered

- Separate Business Needs from Recommended Services.
- Keep Evidence as a first-class concept throughout the analysis pipeline.
- Preserve the current backend architecture while redesigning only the presentation layer.
- Continue evolving Localhost as the primary product interface.
