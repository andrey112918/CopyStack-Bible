## Problems Encountered

### Workspace Organization

As the number of generated artifacts increased, maintaining a predictable directory structure became increasingly important.

---

### Persistent State

Processing state previously existed primarily during runtime.

---

### Localhost Scalability

The growing number of generated artifacts required improvements to navigation and workspace visualization.

The Localhost interface was reorganized to remain usable as the Research Center continues to expand.

---

## Decisions Made

### Workspace-Centric Architecture

A strategic decision was made to organize every Research Job around a dedicated workspace rather than scattered artifacts.

This creates a clear ownership model and simplifies future extensions.

---

### Persistent Research Sessions

Research execution should always generate persistent outputs that remain available after processing has completed.

Future intelligence modules should consume these persisted artifacts rather than recreating them.

---

### Standardized Artifact Layout

All future Research Center components must follow the same directory structure and persistence conventions.

Maintaining consistency across every module reduces future maintenance effort.
