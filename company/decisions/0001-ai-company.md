# Decision 0001

## Title

CopyStack will be built as an AI company, not as a traditional service business.

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

16 June 2026

---

## Context

At the beginning of the project, CopyStack started as a copywriting business providing services manually.

During planning, we realized that building a traditional agency would permanently limit growth.

Instead of scaling through more employees, CopyStack should scale through software and AI systems.

---

## Decision

CopyStack will prioritize building products, AI systems and reusable technology over selling manual services.

Services may exist temporarily, but their purpose is to validate ideas and finance product development.

The final objective is to become a software-first AI company.

---

## Why

Products scale.

Services do not.

Every manual process should eventually become an automated system.

Every successful service should become a product.

---

## Consequences

All future decisions should support long-term product development.

Revenue generated from services will be reinvested into building the CopyStack ecosystem.

---

## Reversible?

NO
