# Decision 0001

## Title

CopyStack will be built as an AI company, not as a traditional service business.

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

16 June 2026

---

## Context

CopyStack initially started as a traditional copywriting service business where every project would be completed manually.

During the early planning phase, it became clear that scaling through additional employees would permanently limit growth, increase operational complexity, and reduce long-term profitability.

The project required a fundamentally different approach—one focused on building software, AI systems, and reusable technology rather than relying on manual work.

---

## Decision

CopyStack will prioritize building products, AI systems, and reusable technology over selling manual services.

Client services may continue to exist during the early stages of the company, but only as a way to validate ideas, understand customer needs, and generate revenue to finance product development.

The long-term objective is to become a software-first AI company whose primary value comes from proprietary technology rather than manual execution.

---

## Why

Software scales.

Manual services do not.

Every manual workflow represents an opportunity to build an AI system.

Every successful service should eventually evolve into a repeatable product that can be delivered automatically.

This strategy enables sustainable growth while continuously increasing the value of the CopyStack ecosystem.

---

## Consequences

Future engineering decisions should prioritize reusable systems over custom client work.

Revenue generated through services should be reinvested into product development.

Every department inside CopyStack should aim to reduce manual work through automation.

The success of the company will be measured primarily by the quality of its products rather than the number of services delivered.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### Traditional Digital Agency

Rejected because growth would depend almost entirely on hiring additional people, increasing operational costs and limiting scalability.

### Freelance Business

Rejected because it creates a dependency on the founder's personal availability and does not produce reusable intellectual property.

### Hybrid Agency Model

Partially accepted only as a temporary transition strategy to finance product development during the early stages of the company.

---

## Reversible?

**NO**

This decision defines the long-term vision of CopyStack and serves as one of the project's core business principles.
