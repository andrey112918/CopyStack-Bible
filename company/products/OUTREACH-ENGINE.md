# Outreach Engine

## Status

**In Active Development**

The Outreach Engine is one of the core products inside the CopyStack AI Employee Operating System.

Its purpose is to help users discover businesses, analyze them, understand their real needs, and eventually generate highly personalized outreach campaigns across multiple communication channels.

The long-term goal is to replace manual prospect research with an intelligent, evidence-backed outreach workflow.

---

# Vision

The Outreach Engine is not a mass messaging tool.

It is an intelligent sales operating system.

Instead of asking:

> "Who can I send a message to?"

It answers:

- Who is worth contacting?
- Why are they worth contacting?
- What problems does the business have?
- What services should CopyStack offer?
- Which outreach channel should be used?
- What should happen next?

Every recommendation should be supported by structured evidence.

# Evidence-First Architecture

Every recommendation must be supported by evidence.

The Outreach Engine never recommends services without explaining why.

Example:

Problem

Missing CTA

Evidence

Homepage contains no visible primary call-to-action.

Confidence

0.91

This evidence becomes reusable by future AI systems.
